Privacy Policy

Last updated — June 2026

Who We Are

fab.games is operated from Hawaii, USA. We make fun, addictive games and creative tools for people around the world. You can reach us at fun@fab.games.

What We Collect

When you sign up for a free trial we collect your email address. That's it. No credit card, no address, no phone number.

When you subscribe to Premium we collect payment information through Stripe, our secure payment processor. We never see or store your full card details.

We may also collect basic usage data such as which games you play, how often you visit, and your general location (country level only) to improve the experience.

How We Use Your Email

We use your email to send you your free trial confirmation, a reminder when your free month is ending, and occasional updates about new games and features. We will never sell your email to anyone. Ever.

You can unsubscribe from any email at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email we send.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies from third parties.

Third Party Services

We use Stripe for payment processing. Their privacy policy can be found at stripe.com/privacy.

We may use basic analytics to understand how people use fab.games. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

Your Rights

You can request a copy of your data, ask us to delete your account, or opt out of any communications at any time. Just email us at fun@fab.games and we'll sort it out promptly.

Children

fab.games is designed for all ages and is family friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information please contact us immediately.

Changes

If we make significant changes to this policy we will notify subscribers by email. Continued use of fab.games after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy? We're friendly. Email us at fun@fab.games and we'll get back to you.
