— Our Story

Built for the Joy
of Playing

fab.games was born in Hawaii with one simple idea — the world needs more fabulous games.

"We believe games should be beautiful, fun and something the whole family can enjoy — wherever in the world they are."

How It Started

It started with a love of Hawaii — the islands, the culture, the spirit of aloha. We wanted to build games that captured that magic and share it with the world.

What began as a small collection of Hawaiian-themed games quickly became something bigger — a vision for a global platform where every country, every culture and every era could be celebrated through play.

What We Build

We build games that are genuinely fun — not pay-to-win, not full of ads, not designed to trap you. Just pure, honest, addictive fun for the whole family.

We also build creative tools like Film Lab — our vintage camera that lets you see the world through the lens of a different era. Because creativity is just another form of play.

Where We're Going

Hawaii is just the beginning. We're building games and experiences inspired by the world's most extraordinary places — Tokyo, Paris, New York, Bali, the African savanna and beyond.

Every month something new arrives at fab.games. A new game, a new film collection, a new corner of the world to explore.
